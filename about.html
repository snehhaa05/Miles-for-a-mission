About Miles for a Mission

Our Mission

Miles for a Mission is India’s premier online marathon event aimed at promoting health, fitness, and community spirit. We bring runners from all over the country together to participate, compete, and contribute to meaningful causes.

Why Participate?

  • Health Benefits: Improve your fitness and well-being.
  • Community Impact: Support charitable causes through your participation.
  • Flexibility: Run anytime, anywhere within the event dates.
  • Recognition: Get featured on our leaderboard and win exciting prizes.

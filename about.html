About Miles for a Mission Marathon

The Miles for a Mission Marathon is India's most energetic celebration of fitness, unity, and community spirit. Since its launch, it has united runners, families, and cities across the country—motivating people to go beyond their limits, support health causes, and make new friends on the track. It’s not just a race; it’s a movement to uplift the nation through healthy competition, inclusivity, and the sheer joy of running together.

  • Date: October 26, 2025 (Kolkata edition)
  • Major Cities: Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata
  • Open to: All ages and abilities. Choose from a full marathon, half marathon, 10K, or 5K fun run.
  • Mission: Uniting India for health, fitness, and hope—one stride at a time.

Kolkata Marathon 2025 - Race Details

The Kolkata Marathon 2025 takes you on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant, historic streets of the City of Joy. Experience scenic running routes past icons like the Victoria Memorial and Howrah Bridge.

  • Date: October 26, 2025
  • Location: Kolkata, West Bengal
  • Start Time: 6:00 AM (Full Marathon)
  • Categories: Full Marathon (42.195 km) Half Marathon (21.097 km) 10K Run 5K Fun Run
  • Reporting Time: 5:00 AM for all categories
  • Bib Collection: Oct 24-25, Netaji Indoor Stadium

What to Expect

  • Hydration points every 2.5 km
  • Medical aid stations every 5 km
  • Live entertainment along the route
  • Finisher medal and e-certificate for all participants
  • Post-race refreshments and recovery zone
